# ethicsWorkshopMaterial
Workshop material on ethics for Forge, a product development club on campus. Made in collaboration with students and faculty members.

Ethical Design Workshop Material is made in collaboration with Dr. Yafeng Wang at Northeastern 

Metaethics workshop was conducted by Dr. John Basl at Northeastern, no material is available in repository. 

The industry workshop was collaborated on with three students at Northeastern. 

The reflection and deconstruction workshop was conducted with student project leads of Forge.
